Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Musico Technologies

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored locally by your browser when you interact with a web application. They enhance functionality, improve loading times, and keep sessions secure between page visits.

2. The Bot Itself

The Musico bot operates natively within Discord and does not inject or utilize web browser cookies on user machines during Discord chat. Your Discord client manages its own authentication independently.

3. The Dashboard

The Musico web dashboard uses strictly necessary cookies to function. These are set when you log in with Discord OAuth2:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
connect.sid Maintains your authenticated Discord session 7 days

No analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies are used. We do not use third-party cookie services.

4. Managing Cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to reject or clear cookies at any time. However, doing so will disable dashboard login functionality, as the session cookie is required to identify you after Discord authorization.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as the dashboard evolves. Any significant changes will be announced in our support server.

  If you have any questions about our cookie usage, please reach out via our Support Server.
